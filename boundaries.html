Resident Responsibilities

There are several items residents can do that will help volunteer fire fighters protect your home and property in the event of a fire.

1. Defenisble Space

Defensible space is, in simplified terms, “room” for the fire fighters to do their jobs. Your house is more likely to withstand a wildfire if grasses, brush, trees, and other common forest fuels are removed, reduced, or modified to reduce a fire’s intensity.

1. Accessibility

Ensure access to the house is clear and kept up so that fire trucks can safely pass. Make sure trees limbs are trimmed to 15ft high and roads are clear from both obstructions and large pot holes.
