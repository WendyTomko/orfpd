What we've been doing

Each month volunteers conduct training. This month training centered around extracting injured persons from their motor vechicles. ORFPD volunteers assist local authorities in traffic accidents, along hwy 101, approximately 10 times a year.

Next month the team will train with the Department of Public Safety Standards Training (DPSST) to ensure the team is fighting fire in accordance with state standards.
